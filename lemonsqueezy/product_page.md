## Pro Features

**AI background removal** — One click strips the background from every image in your batch using a local AI model. No editing, no green screen, no cloud uploads — just clean, transparent subjects ready to frame.

**ICO export** — Output a multi-size Windows ICO file (16, 32, 48, 64, 128, 256 px) in a single run. Drop it straight into your project as an app icon. Free tier outputs PNG only.

**Rounded Square frame shape** — Go beyond circle and square. Rounded Square gives your exports that polished, app-icon aesthetic — with a fully adjustable corner radius (5–50%). Free tier includes Circle and Square.

**Unlimited batch processing** — Free tier processes up to 10 images per run. Pro removes the cap entirely. Drop in hundreds of images and walk away.
